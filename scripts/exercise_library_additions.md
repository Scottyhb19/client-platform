# Odyssey — Exercise Library Additions (candidates)

**Purpose.** Net-new candidates for the loaded library — movements not in your current list, and deliberately *not* the staples you curated out earlier. Review, keep what you want, merge into the master.

**Prescription convention.** 3x8 compound (Push, Pull, Hinge, Squat); 3x12 accessory; Core holds `3x30sec` / reps `3x12`; Carry `3x20m`; jumps `4x5`–`4x3`. Med ball power throws stay on a power scheme (`4x5`), not `3x12`. Format: `Name SETSxREPS METRIC | TAGS`. `-` = bodyweight, `KG` = load, `m` = distance. `(machine)` tagged inline.
